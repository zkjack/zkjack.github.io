# `题目：`

编写一个SQL查询，查找所有至少连续出现三次的数字。

| Id | Num |
|----|-----|
| 1  |  1  |
| 2  |  1  |
| 3  |  1  |
| 4  |  2  |
| 5  |  1  |
| 6  |  2  |
| 7  |  2  |

例如，给定上面的Logs表，1是唯一连续出现至少三次的数字。

| ConsecutiveNums |
|-----------------|
| 1               |

# 方案一：

    SELECT DISTINCT l1.Num AS ConsecutiveNums
    FROM Logs l1, Logs l2, Logs l3
    WHERE l1.Num = l2.Num AND l2.Num = l3.Num AND l1.Id = l2.Id - 1 AND l2.Id = l3.Id - 1;
